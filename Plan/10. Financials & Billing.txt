Financials & Billing – Detailed Breakdown
This module tracks every dollar from bid to closeout with strong finish-trade granularity and California government compliance (AIA forms, certified payroll, retention, prevailing wage, DBE). It is tightly bidirectional with RFP (awarded pricing), Estimating, Scheduling, Documents (AI reviews), and the Unified Takeoff Engine.
Front-end: W3CRM — start from finance.html, ecom-invoice.html, reports.html; add usis-financials.html as unified shell.
1. Core Purpose

Provide live visibility into Budget vs Actual vs Forecast.
Automate change order recovery from AI findings.
Ensure government-ready billing and audit trails.
Turn AI plan reviews into real financial protection.

4. Key Sections & Features
A. Project Financial Header

Contract Value, % Billed, % Complete, Projected Profit %, Retention Held
“AI-Adjusted Forecast” badge (pulls from saved AI reviews)
Large color-coded variance indicator

B. Budget vs Actual Hierarchical Table (Core View)

Bootstrap table or Tabulator/DataTables with tree/group by CSI Division → Trade → Line Item
Columns:
Description (Drywall Hanging, 2nd Coat Paint, etc.)
Original Budget (from Estimating)
Approved COs
Current Budget
Committed (awarded RFP amounts)
Actual Cost (from timesheets + invoices)
Variance $ and %
Forecast to Complete
AI Impact $ (new column from drawing_annotations)

Color coding: Green (under), Red (over, especially AI-triggered), Yellow (watch)
Click row → drill-down or linked drawing

C. AI Variance & Risk Panel (Right Sidebar)

AI offcanvas (usis-ai-drawer.js) in financial_review mode (Local Llama 4 Scout default)
Live cards from saved AI reviews:
“Critical fire separation on A2.1 → +$18k drywall labor / +4 days”
“Substrate moisture flagged → recommend +$9.2k”

One-click “Create Change Order from this Finding”
What-If sliders

D. Billing & Invoicing Workflow

Progress Billing Schedule (SOV) table
AIA G702/G703 form generation (Flask Jinja2 backend → professional PDF)
Auto-population from Estimating + RFP + % complete from Scheduling
Retention tracking (5% or 10%)
Certified payroll upload & validation

E. Change Order Management

Fully automated flow triggered by AI:
AI review flags issue → “Create CO” button
Pre-filled: Description (AI text + citation), Cost impact, Supporting evidence (annotated drawing)
Approval workflow with audit trail

On approval: auto-updates Budget, Schedule, Estimating, and notifies via existing email system

F. Profitability & Forecasting

Per-trade profitability dashboard (Drywall vs Paint vs Flooring)
Cash flow forecast chart with AI risk overlays
Margin by GC / Project type

5. Data Layer (SQLAlchemy – Sage-Aligned)

Budget + BudgetLineItem (hierarchical, versioned)
Commitment + CommitmentLineItem (from RFP)
ChangeOrder (with ai_triggered flag + FK to DrawingAnnotation)
Invoice, Payment, FinancialTransaction
Strong relationships to TakeoffLineItem, RFPResponse, Task, DrawingAnnotation, DailyLog

6. Critical Integrations (Preserve 100%)

RFP Module: Awarded pricing becomes commitments automatically
Unified Takeoff / Estimating: Baseline budget created on award; variances flow both ways
Scheduling: % complete and delays update forecasts and billing
DrawingViewer + Local AI + aiReviewBus.js: Every saved review can trigger variance alerts and CO suggestions
Safety: Safety corrective work flows into COs and Budget
Documents Hub: All COs, invoices, payroll docs stored with AI evidence

Photo receipt uploads with queued AI cost extraction
