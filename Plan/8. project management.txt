Project Management & Field Operations – Detailed Breakdown
This module turns architectural plans into daily crew execution for your finish-work trades (drywall, painting, flooring, ceilings, trim). It is heavily integrated with DrawingViewer, Local AI, RFP (awarded subs), Unified Takeoff, Safety, and Financials. Field crews get a simple mobile-first experience with offline support; office teams get real-time visibility and AI-driven adjustments.
Canonical PM + field execution spec: Plan 8 owns Gantt/task board, daily logs, punch lists, and DrawingViewer sidebar workflows. Plan 9 (Scheduling & Field Ops) adds calendar-first scheduling emphasis; implement shared pages once (usis-field.html, usis-schedule.html) and link both plans there — see Plan\0 §5.
Front-end shell: W3CRM (Bootstrap + vanilla JS). Starter pages: project.html, task.html, app-calender.html, performance.html.
1. Core Purpose

Convert takeoffs and drawings into scheduled, trackable tasks.
Enable seamless field data capture (daily logs, production, safety, photos).
Close the loop between plans, execution, and financials.
Use Local AI to reduce coordination errors and delays.

4. Key Sections & Features
A. Schedule Header & Overview

Overall timeline progress bar with % complete
Critical Path highlights (red if AI-flagged delays)
Key milestones with AI-adjusted dates
Trade breakdown chips (planned vs actual production)
“Generate AI-Adjusted Schedule” button (analyzes all saved AI reviews)

B. Interactive Gantt & Task Management

Tasks grouped by trade and phase
Columns: Task Name, Duration, Start/End, Assigned Crew/Sub (from RFP), % Complete, Dependencies, AI Risk Badge
Drag-and-drop rescheduling
Click task → loads related drawing in right sidebar + opens AI panel

C. AI-Powered Task Intelligence (Right Sidebar)

AI offcanvas (usis-ai-drawer.js) in field_review / scheduling_review mode
Quick buttons:
“Review This Task’s Drawing with Local AI”
“Suggest Sequence Adjustments”
“Add Coordination Days for Flagged Issues”

AI proposes extra days for substrate repairs, fire caulking, MEP coordination, etc.
One-click “Apply Suggestion” → updates Gantt + audit log

D. Field Operations Tools (Mobile-First)

Daily Logs:
**Daily pretask (project pretask)** — first step in the daily field flow (Plan 7 §10): links to **project JHA** revision, scope/weather/PPE/sign-offs; may be **required** before production quantities save (configurable). Embedded via `elements/usis-daily-pretask-form.html` in daily log / `usis-field.html`.
Crew size, hours worked, production quantities per trade
Weather / delays
Photo upload → auto-triggers Local AI review (safety/quality)
GPS timestamp + signature

Punch List:
Create items with photo + location on DrawingViewer
AI-assisted severity & category
Assign to crew or subcontractor (links to RFP)
Status workflow + before/after photos

Timesheets & Production Tracking:
Offline-capable
Real-time sync to Financials for labor cost tracking

Field Photo → AI Flow:
Camera button → photo → optional alignment to drawing → sent to Local AI for as-built vs plan review


E. Task Templates Library

Pre-built finish-trade templates (e.g., “Standard 3-Coat Paint Package”)
“AI Generate from Drawings” button that scans saved AI reviews and builds logical sequence + durations

6. Critical Integrations (Preserve 100%)

DrawingViewer: Task click loads exact sheet + annotations
Unified Takeoff: Quantities → task durations and crew sizing
Local AI + aiReviewBus.js: Reviews update risk badges and suggest new tasks
RFP Module: Awarded subs auto-create tasks with pricing
Safety Management: **Project JHAs** (Plan 7 §9); **daily pretask** embedded before or alongside daily safety checklist in Daily Logs
Financials: % complete updates billing and variance
Documents Hub: All logs, photos, punch lists stored centrally
