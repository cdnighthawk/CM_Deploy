Scheduling & Field Ops – Detailed Breakdown
This module turns architectural plans and takeoffs into daily crew execution for your finish-work trades (drywall, painting, flooring, ceilings, trim). It is heavily integrated with DrawingViewer, Local AI, RFP (awarded subs), Unified Takeoff Engine, Safety, and Financials. Field crews get a simple mobile-first experience with offline support; office teams get real-time visibility and AI-driven adjustments.
Relationship to Plan 8: Plan 9 emphasizes scheduling calendars, sequencing, and calendar-driven field coordination. Plan 8 remains the canonical home for shared field artifacts (daily logs, punch lists, production capture). Do not duplicate two conflicting UIs — reuse usis-schedule.html / usis-field.html described in Plan 8; extend with calendar-centric views from app-calender.html (Plan\0).
1. Core Purpose

Convert takeoffs into scheduled, assignable tasks.
Capture accurate field production and safety data daily.
Use Local AI to suggest adjustments and reduce coordination errors.
Feed production data back into Budget, Financials, and Estimating for accurate forecasting.

4. Key Sections & Features
A. Schedule Header & Overview

Overall timeline progress bar with % complete
Critical Path highlights (red if AI-flagged delays)
Key milestones with AI-adjusted dates
Trade breakdown chips showing planned vs actual production
“Generate AI-Adjusted Schedule” button (analyzes all saved drawing_annotations)

B. Interactive Gantt & Task Management

Tasks grouped by trade and phase
Columns: Task Name (e.g., “Level 3 Drywall Hanging”), Duration, Start/End dates, Assigned Crew/Sub (from RFP), % Complete, Dependencies, AI Risk Badge
Drag-and-drop rescheduling and resizing
Click task → loads related drawing in right sidebar + opens AI panel

C. AI-Powered Task Intelligence (Right Sidebar)

AI offcanvas via usis-ai-drawer.js in field_review / scheduling_review mode (Local Llama 4 Scout default)
Quick buttons:
“Review This Task’s Drawing with Local AI”
“Suggest Sequence Adjustments”
“Add Coordination Days for Flagged Issues”

AI automatically proposes extra days for substrate repairs, fire caulking, MEP coordination, etc.
One-click “Apply Suggestion” → updates Gantt + audit log

D. Field Operations Tools (Mobile-First)

Daily Logs:
Crew size, hours worked, production quantities per trade
Weather / delays / notes
Photo upload → auto-triggers Local AI review (safety/quality/compliance)
GPS timestamp + signature capture

Punch List:
Create items with photo + location pin on DrawingViewer
AI-assisted severity & category
Assign to crew or subcontractor (links to RFP vendors)
Status workflow: Open → In Progress → Resolved (with before/after photos)

Timesheets & Production Tracking:
Offline-capable entry
Real-time sync to Financials for labor cost tracking
Production units feed back into Estimating for benchmarking


E. Task Templates Library

Pre-built finish-trade templates
“AI Generate from Drawings” button that scans saved AI reviews and builds logical sequence + durations

5. Data Layer (SQLAlchemy)

Task / Schedule models
Strong relationships to Project, TakeoffLineItem, RFPResponse, Drawing, DrawingAnnotation, DailyLog
DailyLog, PunchListItem, FieldPhoto
TaskDependency

6. Critical Integrations (Preserve 100%)

DrawingViewer: Task click loads exact sheet + annotations
Unified Takeoff Engine: Quantities → task durations and crew sizing
Local AI + aiReviewBus.js: Reviews update risk badges and suggest new tasks
RFP Module: Awarded subs auto-create tasks with pricing
Safety Management: Daily safety checklist embedded in Daily Logs
Financials: % complete updates billing and variance
Documents Hub: All logs, photos, punch lists stored centrally

7. Mobile-First & Offline Behavior

Bottom nav emphasizes Field view
Full offline support via IndexedDB: create/edit tasks, logs, photos, punch lists
Local AI review queue (process when back online)
Large touch targets, simplified Gantt (list + calendar fallback on mobile)

8. Government & Compliance Features

Prevailing wage tracking in daily logs
